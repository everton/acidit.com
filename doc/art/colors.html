Inspiration

The main theme inspiration was the most urban scenarios around the current world and some distopic futuristics components from fictional Cyberpunk stories.

The background and text colors are extracted or manipulated from colors of a night shot of Hong Kong city.

Highlighted content must be created through usage of "neon effects".

Form epxerience mimics the visual identity of Tron movie.
